Isaac Childres

Gloomhaven, Forge War, Founders of Gloomhaven, and other titles

Game designer and a one-man army of board game design and development.

Eric M. Lang

Blood Rage, Warhammer 40,000: Conquest, and other titles

Game designer. He began his career as a playtester for FASA before publishing his first game, Mystick independently in 2000.

Jacob Fryxelius

Terraforming Mars, After The Virus, and other titles

Truly a veteran gamer and responsible for much of the gaming influence in the family. A creative game designer who is mainly focused on card games.

Michael Kiesling

Azul, Mexica, Tikal, and other titles

German board game designer. Many of his games have been nominated for or have won the Spiel des Jahres, a German games award.

Rob Daviau

Axis & Allies: Pacific , Pandemic Legacy, Return to Dark Tower, and other titles

American game designer known for creating legacy board gaming. Lectured at Massachusetts Institute of Technology, and New York University on game design

The Dice Tower
