Tournaments Inscription

  • The teams play against time, the team that defeats the game in the shortest time will be the winner. In case the team loses, it will leave the competition.
  • When you sign up for yourself or your team in a tournament, entry to the convention will be free every day until the closing ceremony.
  • The number of people per team is determined by the maximum number of players per board game. (eg. Gloomheaven - 4 people, Pandemic - 4 people)
  • Every tournament starts at 10:00 A.M. The second round will start at 3:00 P.M. And the finalists will be decided in the last round, at 8:00 P.M.
  • The finalists of every game will compete on the final day at 4:00 P.M.
  • For more details about fees and prizes, please read these guidelines carefully
